Predicate: terminusOf

Summary

(terminusOf x e)

e is a change in an “at” relation and x is the location of the final “at” relation.

Characterizing Axioms

An instrument is an entity that the agent causes to go through a change of state, where this change plays an intermediate role in the causal chain.

(forall (w e)
  (iff (terminusOf w e)
       (exists (x z e1 e2 s)
         (and (at' e1 x z s) (at' e2 x w s) (change' e e1 e2)))))
