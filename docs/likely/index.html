Predicate: likely

(likely e c)

e is likely, given constraints c.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (e c)
  (iff (likely e c)
       (exists (s d s1)
         (and (likelihood d e c) (likelihoodScale s) (Hi s1 s)
              (inScale d s1)))))
