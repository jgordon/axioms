Predicate: difficult

Summary

(difficult e a)

Action e is difficult for agent a.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (a e)
  (if (difficult e a)
      (and (agent a) (agentOf a e))))

(forall (e1 e a)
  (iff (difficult' e1 e a)
       (and (agent a) (agentOf a e)
            (exists (s)
              (and (difficultyScale s e) (scaleFor s e1))))))
