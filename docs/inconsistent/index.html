Predicate: inconsistent

Summary

(inconsistent s1 s2)

s1 and s2 are inconsistent sets of eventualities.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (s1 s2)
  (iff (inconsistent s1 s2)
       (and (eventualities s1) (eventualities s2)
            (exists (e1 e2)
              (and (imply s1 e1) (imply s2 e2) (not' e2 e1))))))

Additional Axioms

If two states are not inconsistent, then the change must be a composite of two changes, one to and the other from an inconsistent state.

(forall (e e1 e2)
  (if (change' e e1 e2)
      (or (inconsistent e1 e2)
          (exists (e3 e4 e5 e6)
            (and (change' e4 e1 e3) (change' e5 e3 e2) (and' e6 e4 e5)
                 (gen e6 e))))))
