Predicate: inconsistent

Summary

(inconsistent s1 s2)

s1 and s2 are inconsistent sets of eventualities.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (s1 s2)
  (iff (inconsistent s1 s2)
       (and (eventualities s1) (eventualities s2)
            (exists (e1 e2)
              (and (imply s1 e1) (imply s2 e2) (not' e2 e1))))))

Additional Axioms

(forall (e e1 e2)
  (if (change' e e1 e2)
      (or (inconsistent e1 e2)
          (exists (e3 e4 e5 e6)
            (and (change' e4 e1 e3) (change' e5 e3 e2) (and' e6 e4 e5)
                 (gen e6 e))))))

(forall (e e1 e2)
  (if (and (change e1 e2) (etc))
      (inconsistent e1 e2)))

(forall (e e1 e3)
  (if (subst e3 e3 e1 e1)
      (iff (changeFrom' e e1)
           (exists (e2 e5)
             (and (change' e5 e1 e2) (gen e5 e) (inconsistent e3 e2))))))

(forall (e e2 e4)
  (if (subst e4 e4 e2 e2)
      (iff (changeTo' e e2)
           (exists (e1 e5)
             (and (change' e5 e1 e2) (gen e5 e) (inconsistent e4 e1))))))
