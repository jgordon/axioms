Predicate: body

Summary

(body b p)

b is p's body.

Characterizing Axioms

A body is a physical object.

(forall (b p)
  (if (body b p)
      (physobj b)))

A body is a composite entity whose components are body parts. In fact, it's a spatial system, since all the body parts are physical objects.

(forall (b p)
  (if (body b p)
      (and (spatialSystem b)
           (exists (s)
             (and (componentsOf s b)
                  (forall (x)
                    (iff (member x s) (bodyPart x p))))))))

Additional Axioms

A person has a body and a mind.

(forall (p)
  (if (person p)
      (exists (b m)
        (and (body b p) (mind m p)))))
