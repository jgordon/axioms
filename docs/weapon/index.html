Predicate: weapon

Summary

(weapon w)

w is a weapon

Additional Axioms

Adversaries use weapons in attacks.

(forall (e1 x y)
  (if (attack e1 x y)
      (exists (w z)
        (and (use e1 x w y z) (weapon w)))))

(forall (e1 w x y z)
  (if (and (use e1 x w y z) (weapon w))
      (use-weapon e1 x w y z)))

(forall (e1 w x y z)
  (if (use-weapon e1 x w y z)
      (exists (u)
        (and (use-weapon e1 w u y z) (weapon w)))))
