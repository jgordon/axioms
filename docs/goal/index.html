Predicate: goal

Summary

(goal e g x)

g is a goal of x.

Characterizing Axioms

Adversaries have contradictory goals.

(forall (e g x)
  (if (goal e g x)
      (exists (y h)
        (conflict c x y g h))))

(forall (e1 g x)
  (if (goal e1 g x)
      (goal-nn e1 g)))

(forall (e1 x g u)
  (if (goal e1 g x)
      (want-vb e1 x g u)))

(forall (e1 e2 g x)
  (if (goal e1 g x)
      (and (goal-nn e1 g) (of-in e2 g x))))

An adversary has a strategy The relationship between the plan and the goals is unclear, but one is evidence for the other.

(forall (e e1 g x u1)
  (if (goal e1 g x)
      (plan-nn e u1)))

(forall (e1 g x u1)
  (if (goal e1 g x)
      (strategy-nn e u1)))
