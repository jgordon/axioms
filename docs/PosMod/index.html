Predicate: PosMod

Summary

(PosMod e p)

The positive modality p holds of eventuality e.

Characterizing Axioms

The predicate PosMod is a cover term for all positive modalities. Its second argument will be a predicate that labels the modality.

(forall (e p) (if (PosMod e p) (predicate p)))

Additional Axioms

Rexist is a positive modality.

(forall (e) (if (Rexist e) (PosMod e Rexist)))

The principal property of positive modalities is that Modus Ponens can be applied within the modality.

(forall (e1 e2 m)
  (if (and (PosMod e1 m) (imply e1 e2))
      (PosMod e2 m)))

Possibility is a positive modality.

(forall (e c)
  (if (possible e c)
      (PosMod e possible)))

Necessity is a positive modality because of the transitivity of imply.

(forall (e c)
  (if (necessary e c)
      (PosMod e necessary)))
