Predicate: forAllOfSeq

Summary

(forAllOfSeq e s x e1)

e is the event consisting of iterations of event type e1 where in successive iterations the role of x is played by the successive members of sequence s.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (e s e1)
  (iff (forAllOfSeq e s x e1)
       (exists (y e2 l e3 s1)
         (or (and (length 1 s) (first y s) (subst y e2 x e1) (equal e e2))
             (and (length l s) (gt l 1) (first y s) (subst y e2 x e1)
                  (eventSequence e e2 e3) (forAllOfSeq e3 s1 x e1)
                  (rest s1 s))))))
