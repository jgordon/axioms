Predicate: begins

Summary

(begins t1 t2)

Instant t1 is the beginning of t2.

Characterizing Axioms

A temporal entity may be an instant or an interval.

(forall (t1 t2)
  (if (begins t1 t2)
      (and (instant t1)
           (or (temporalEntity t2) (tseq t2) (eventuality t2)))))
