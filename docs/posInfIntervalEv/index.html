Predicate: posInfIntervalEv

Summary

(posInfIntervalEv e)

e is a positive infinite interval or an eventuality whose time span is a positive infinite interval.

Characterizing Axioms

A temporal entity may be an instant or an interval.

(forall (e)
  (iff (posInfIntervalEv e)
       (or (posInfInterval e)
           (exists (t)
             (and (timeSpanOf t e) (posInfInterval t))))))

Additional Axioms

A temporal entity may be an instant or an interval.

(forall (e)
  (iff (properIntervalEv e)
       (or (properInterval e)
           (exists (t)
             (and (timeSpanOf t e) (properInterval t)))
           (posInfIntervalEv e))))

A temporal entity may be an instant or an interval.

(forall (t1 t2)
  (iff (intFinishes t1 t2)
       (and (properIntervalEv t1) (properIntervalEv t2)
            (exists (t3 t4)
              (and (begins t3 t1) (begins t4 t2) (before t4 t3)))
            (or (exists (t)
                  (and (ends t t1) (ends t t2)))
                (and (posInfIntervalEv t1)
                     (posInfIntervalEv t2))))))
