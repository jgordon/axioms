Predicate: gen

Summary

(gen e1 e2)

e1 generates or entails the existence of e2.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (e1 e2)
  (if (gen e1 e2)
      (and (eventuality e1) (eventuality e2))))

If (gen e1 e2) holds, then e1 and e2 occupy the same chunk of space-time:

(forall (e1 e2 t)
  (if (gen e1 e2)
      (iff (atTime e1 t)
           (atTime e2 t))))

(forall (e1 e2 x s)
  (if (gen e1 e2)
      (iff (atLoc e1 x s)
           (atLoc e2 x s))))

Additional Axioms

(forall (e) (not (gen e e)))
