Predicate: addElt

Summary

(addElt s s1 x)

s is obtained from s1 by adding x.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (s s1 x)
  (iff (addElt s s1 x)
       (and (set s) (set s1)
            (forall (y)
              (iff (member y s)
                   (or (equal y x) (member y s1)))))))

Additional Axioms

(forall (s x)
  (iff (singleton s x)
       (exists (s1)
         (and (addElt s s1 x) (null s1)))))
