Predicate: over

Characterizing Axioms

A composite entity can be over a region.

(forall (z  s)
   (iff (over z r s)
        (and (compositeEntity z) (region r) (subset r s)
             (forall (x)
                (if (componentOf x z)
                    (exists (y)
                       (and (inside y r) (at x y s))))))))
