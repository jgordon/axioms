Predicate: repeatUntil

Summary

(repeatUntil e e1 e2)

e is the event consisting of iterations of event type e1 happening until e2 happens or holds.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (e e1 e2)
  (iff (repeatUntil e e1 e2)
       (exists (e3 e4 e5)
         (and (eventSequence' e e3 e4) (instance e3 e1) (cond e4 e2 e5)
              (or (repeatUntil e5 e1 e2) (instance e5 e1))))))
