Predicate: quill

Characterizing Axioms

It will be useful to have a name for a ray going from p0 through p1 and p. We will call this a “quill”, after the quills of porcupines.

(forall (c v r)
   (iff (quill c v r)
        (exists (p0 p1 p)
           (and (volume v) (face r v p) (ray c) (inside p c)
                (endpoint p0 c) (centralIn p0 v)
                (centralIn p1 r) (inside p1 c))))))

Additional Axioms

The orientation of a volume v with a designated face r in a framework f is the direction of a ray c from a central point in v passing through a central point in r, i.e., the direction of a quill.

(forall (d v r f)
   (iff (orientation0 d v r f)
        (exists (c)
           (and (quill c v r) (directionOf d c f)))))
