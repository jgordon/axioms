Predicate: propertiesOf

Summary

(propertiesOf s2 x)

s2 is the set of properties of composite entity x.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (s2 x)
  (if (propertiesOf s2 x)
      (and (set s2) (compositeEntity x)
           (forall (e)
             (if (member e s2)
                 (exists (y)
                   (and (componentOrWhole y x)
                        (onlyarg* y e))))))))
