Predicate: propertiesOf

Summary

(propertiesOf s2 x)

s2 is the set of properties of composite entity x.

Characterizing Axioms

onlyarg* that is either a component of the composite entity or the whole.

(forall (s2 x)
  (if (propertiesOf s2 x)
      (and (set s2) (compositeEntity x)
           (forall (e)
             (if (member e s2)
                 (exists (y)
                   (and (componentOrWhole y x)
                        (onlyarg* y e))))))))

Additional Axioms

(forall (x)
  (iff (compositeEntity x)
       (exists (s1 s2 s3)
         (and (componentsOf s1 x) (propertiesOf s2 x)
              (relationsOf s3 x)))))
