Predicate: impossible

Summary

(impossible e c)

e is impossible with respect to a set of constraints c.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (e c)
  (iff (impossible e c)
       (and (eventuality e) (eventualities c) (not (possible e c)))))

Additional Axioms

(forall (d e c s)
  (if (and (likelihood d e c) (likelihoodScale s))
      (iff (bottom d s)
           (impossible e c))))
