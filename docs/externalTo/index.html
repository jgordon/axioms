Predicate: externalTo

Summary

(externalTo y x)

Neither y nor any of its components is equal to x or one of its components.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (y x)
  (iff (externalTo y x)
       (not (exists (y1 x1)
              (and (componentOrWhole y1 y)
                   (componentOrWhole x1 x)
                   (equal y1 x1))))))

All Axioms

(forall (a x m)
  (if (and (perceive a x) (mind m a))
      (and (agent a) (externalTo x m))))

  (if (at x y s)
      (and (componentOf y s) (externalTo x s))))
