Predicate: beforeOrMeets

Summary

(beforeOrMeets t1 t2)

The end of t1 is before or equal to the beginning of t2.

Characterizing Axioms

A temporal entity may be an instant or an interval.

(forall (t1 t2)
  (iff (beforeOrMeets t1 t2)
       (exists (t3 t4)
         (and (ends t3 t1) (begins t4 t2)
              (or (before t3 t4) (equal t3 t4))))))

Additional Axioms

(forall (e e1 e2)
  (iff (eventSequence e e1 e2)
       (and (event e1) (event e2) (and' e e1 e2) (beforeOrMeets e1 e2))))
