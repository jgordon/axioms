Predicate: horizontal3

Characterizing Axioms

A subset of the domain is horizontal3 if it is either horizontal or vertical2.

(forall (s f)
   (iff (horizontal3 s f)
        (or (horizontal s f) (vertical2 s f))))

Additional Axioms

horizontal3 and vertical3 sets are independent scales.

(forall (s1 s2 f)
   (if (and (framework f) (horizontal3 s1 f) (vertical3 s2 f))
       (independentScales s1 s2)))
