Predicate: nearer

Characterizing Axioms

We can define qualitative terms like "near" and "far" as outlined in Gordon and Hobbs, Chapter 12, and exemplified numerous times in the rest of the book. We first define the partial ordering "nearer".

(forall (x y z)
   (iff (nearer x y z)
        (exists (d1 d2)
           (and (distance d1 x z u) (distance d2 y z u)
                (lt d1 d2)))))

Additional Axioms

Then "nearness" scale is a scale whose partial ordering is nearer.

(forall (s z)
   (iff (nearnessScale s z)
        (exists (s0 e x y u)
           (and (nearer' e x y z u) (scaleDefinedBy s s0 e)))))
