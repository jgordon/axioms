Predicate: partiallyCause

Summary

(partiallyCause e1 e2)

e1 together with something else causes e2.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (e1 e2)
  (iff (partiallyCause e1 e2)
       (exists (e3 e4)
         (and (not (cause e1 e2)) (not (cause e3 e2))
              (and' e4 e1 e3) (cause e4 e2)))))
