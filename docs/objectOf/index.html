Predicate: objectOf

Summary

(objectOf x e)

x is the entity undergoing change in e.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (x e)
  (iff (objectOf x e)
       (or (changeIn' e x)
           (exists (e1 e2)
             (and (and' e e1 e2) (cause e1 e2) (objectOf x e2))))))
