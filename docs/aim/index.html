Predicate: aim

Summary

(aim e x w y)

x aims weapon w at y

Characterizing Axioms

Aim a weapon at a target.

(forall (e1 w x y)
  (if (aim e1 x w y)
      (exists (u)
        (aim-vb e1 x w u))))

(forall (e1 w x y)
  (if (aim e1 x w y)
      (target-vb e1 x y w)))

(forall (e1 w x y)
  (if (aim e1 x w y)
      (crosshairs-nn e1 w)))

To train a weapon on a target is to aim at the target.

(forall (e1 w x y)
  (if (aim e1 x w y)
      (exists (e2 u)
        (and (train-vb e1 x w u) (on-in e2 e1 y)))))

(forall (e1 x w y)
  (if (aim e1 x w y)
      (exists (e2 u)
        (and (aim-vb e1 x w u) (at-in e2 e1 y)))))

Additional Axioms

Aiming is preparation for attack.

(forall (e x y a)
  (if (and (prepare e x a) (attack e x y))
      (exists (w)
        (aim e x w y))))
