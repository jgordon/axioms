Predicate: turnThru

Characterizing Axioms

It will be useful to have a predicate for turning through an angle. Here v is the angle formed by x's from- and to-orientations.

(forall (e x v)
   (iff (turnThru' e x v)
        (exists (d1 d2 o)
           (and (turn' e x d1 d2) (atLoc x o) (vertex v d1 d2 o)))))

Additional Axioms

Two turnThru actions compose into a single turnThru.

(forall (e1 e2 x v1 v2)
   (if (and (turnThru' e1 x v1) (turnThru' e2 x v2))
       (exists (v e)
          (and (addVertex v v1 v2) (turnThru' e x v)))))
