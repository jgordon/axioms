Predicate: spatialUnit

Additional Axioms

We take distance to be a predicate with four arguments – a nonnegative number, two points, and a unit.

(forall (d x1 x2 u)
  (if (distance d x1 x2 u)
      (and (nonNegativeNumber d) (point x1) (point x2)
           (spatialUnit u))))
