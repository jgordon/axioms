Predicate: componentOrWhole

Summary

(componentOrWhole y x)

y is either a component of x or x itself.

Characterizing Axioms

componentOf and being equal to the whole.

(forall (x1 x)
  (iff (componentOrWhole x1 x)
       (or (equal x1 x) (componentOf x1 x))))

Additional Axioms

(forall (y x)
  (iff (externalTo y x)
       (not (exists (y1 x1)
              (and (componentOrWhole y1 y)
                   (componentOrWhole x1 x)
                   (equal y1 x1))))))
