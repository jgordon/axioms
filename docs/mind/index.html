Predicate: mind

Summary

(mind m p)

m is p's mind.

Characterizing Axioms

The mind is a composite entity.

(forall (m p)
  (if (mind m p)
      (compositeEntity m)))

Additional Axioms

A person has a body and a mind.

(forall (p)
  (if (person p)
      (exists (b m)
        (and (body b p) (mind m p)))))
