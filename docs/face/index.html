Predicate: face

Additional Axioms

A face of a polyhedron is one of the faces.

(forall (p s f)
   (if (polyhedron p s)
       (iff (face f p) (member f s))))

A face of a polyhedron is one of the faces.

(forall (c p s)
   (if (polyhedron p s)
       (iff (edge c p)
            (exists (f) (and (face f p) (sideOf c f))))))
