Predicate: ends

Summary

(ends t1 t2)

Instant t1 is the end of t2.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (t1 t2)
  (if (ends t1 t2)
      (and (instant t1)
           (or (temporalEntity t2) (tseq t2) (eventuality t2)))))

Additional Axioms

(forall (t1 t2)
  (if (and (instant t2) (ends t1 t2))
      (equal t1 t2)))
