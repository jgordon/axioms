Predicate: fraction

Summary

(fraction f a b)

f is the fraction whose numerator is a and whose denominator is b; f = a/b.

Characterizing Axioms

A non-negative integer n1 is less than n2 iff there is some positive integer n3 s.t. n2 is the sum of n1 and n3.

(forall (f a b)
  (if (fraction f a b) (number f)))

A non-negative integer n1 is less than n2 iff there is some positive integer n3 s.t. n2 is the sum of n1 and n3.

(forall (f a b)
  (if (fraction f a b)
      (and (number a) (number b) (nequal b 0))))
