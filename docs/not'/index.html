Predicate: not'

Summary

(not' e1 e2)

e1 is the eventuality of e2 not existing.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (e1 e2)
  (if (not' e1 e2)
      (and (eventuality e1) (eventuality e2)
           (iff (Rexist e1)
                (not (Rexist e2))))))

Additional Axioms

(forall (e1 e2)
  (if (nott' e1 e2)
      (exists (e3)
        (and (instance e2 e3) (not' e1 e3)))))
