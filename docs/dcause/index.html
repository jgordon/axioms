Predicate: dcause

Summary

(dcause e1 e2)

Eventuality or agent e1 directly causes eventuality e2 without any intermediate causes.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (e1 e2)
  (iff (dcause e1 e2)
       (and (cause e1 e2)
            (not (exists (e3)
                   (and (cause e1 e3) (cause e3 e2)))))))
