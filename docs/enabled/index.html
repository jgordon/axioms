Predicate: enabled

Summary

(enabled s e t)

All the enabling conditions for causal complex s resulting in eventuality e hold at time t.

Characterizing Axioms

We will say that a causal complex for an eventuality is enabled at time t if all its preconditions hold at time t.

(forall (s e t)
  (iff (enabled s e t)
       (forall (e1)
         (if (enable0 e1 e s) (atTime e1 t)))))

Additional Axioms

If an agent can directly cause an action that is enabled, then it is executable. Moreover, if the action can be caused by another executable action, then it is executable.

(forall (e a c t)
  (iff (executable e a c t)
       (exists (s)
         (and (enabled s e t)
              (or (exists (e1)
                    (and (dcause' e1 a e) (possible e1 c)))
                  (exists (e2)
                    (and (cause e2 e)
                         (executable e2 a c t))))))))
