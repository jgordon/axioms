Predicate: posInfInterval

Summary

(posInfInterval t)

t is a positive infinite interval.

Characterizing Axioms

A temporal entity may be an instant or an interval.

(forall (t)
  (iff (posInfInterval t)
       (and (interval t)
            (not (exists (t2) (ends t2 t))))))
