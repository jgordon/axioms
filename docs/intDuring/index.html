Predicate: intDuring

Summary

(intDuring t1 t2)

Interval t1 begins after and ends before interval t2.

Characterizing Axioms

A temporal entity may be an instant or an interval.

(forall (t1 t2)
  (iff (intDuring t1 t2)
       (and (properIntervalEv t1) (properIntervalEv t2)
            (exists (t3 t4)
              (and (begins t3 t1) (begins t4 t2) (before t4 t3)))
            (exists (t5)
              (and (ends t5 t1)
                   (forall (t6)
                     (if (ends t6 t2) (before t5 t6))))))))
