Predicate: top

Summary

(top x s)

x is the highest element in the scale s.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (x s)
   (iff (top x s)
        (and (inScale x s)
             (forall (y)
               (if (inScale y s) (leqs y x s))))))

The expression (top r v f) says that region r is the top of volume v in framework f.

(forall (r v f)
   (iff (top r v f)
        (exists (r1 c c1 a a1)
           (and (front r1 v f) (face r v p)
                (quill c1 r1 v) (quill c r v f)
                (directionOf a1 c1 f) (directionOf a c f)
                (posYAxis a1 f) (posZAxis a f)))))

Additional Axioms

(forall (s1 s)
   (if (Hi s1 s)
       (forall (x)
         (iff (top x s) (top x s1)))))

If the likelihood of an eventuality is the top of the likelihood scale given constraints c, then it is entailed by c.

(forall (d e c s)
  (if (and (likelihood d e c) (likelihoodScale s))
      (iff (top d s) (necessary e c))))
