Predicate: necessary

Summary

(necessary e c)

e is necessary with respect to a set of constraints c.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (e c)
  (if (necessary e c)
      (PosMod e necessary)))

(forall (e c)
  (if (necessary e c)
      (and (eventuality e) (eventualities c))))

(forall (e c)
  (iff (necessary e c)
       (imply c e)))
