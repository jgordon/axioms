Predicate: and'

Summary

(and' e e1 e2)

e is the eventuality of both e1 and e2 existing.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (e e1 e2)
  (if (and' e e1 e2)
      (and (eventuality e) (eventuality e1) (eventuality e2)
           (iff (Rexist e)
                (and (Rexist e1) (Rexist e2))))))
