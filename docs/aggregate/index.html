Predicate: aggregate

Summary

(aggregate x y z)

x is a composite entity whose components are y and z.

Characterizing Axioms

An aggregate of two entities is a composite entity with those two entities as its components.

(forall (x y z)
  (iff (aggregate x y z)
       (and (compositeEntity x)
            (exists (s)
              (and (componentsOf s x) (doubleton s y z))))))
