Predicate: predicate

Summary

(predicate p)

p is a predicate.

All Axioms

The pred of an eventuality is a predicate.

(forall (e p)
  (if (pred p e)
      (and (predicate p) (eventuality e))))

The predicate PosMod is a cover term for all positive modalities. Its second argument will be a predicate that labels the modality.

(forall (e p) (if (PosMod e p) (predicate p)))
