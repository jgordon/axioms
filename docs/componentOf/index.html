Predicate: componentOf

Summary

(componentOf y x)

y is a component of x.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (y x)
  (iff (componentOf y x)
       (and (compositeEntity x)
            (exists (s)
              (and (componentsOf s x) (member y s))))))

Additional Axioms

componentOf and being equal to the whole.

(forall (x1 x)
  (iff (componentOrWhole x1 x)
       (or (equal x1 x) (componentOf x1 x))))
