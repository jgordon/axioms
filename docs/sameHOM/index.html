Predicate: sameHOM

Summary

(sameHOM x y s1 m)

Two elements x and y of a scale s1 are of the same half order of magnitude under measure m.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (x y s1 m)
  (if (sameHOM x y s1 m)
      (and (inScale x s1) (inScale y s1) (measure m s1))))

Additional Axioms

Are two entites the same half order of magnitude? (Is the proportion of the smaller to the larger less than the square root of 10?)

(forall (x y s1 m)
  (if (and (leqs x y s1) (measure m s1))
      (iff (sameHOM x y s1 m)
           (exists (f f2)
             (and (proportion f y x m) (product f2 f f)
                  (leq f2 10))))))
