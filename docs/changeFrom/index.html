Predicate: changeFrom

Summary

(changeFrom e1)

There is a change out of eventuality e1.

Additional Axioms

(forall (e e1 e3)
  (if (subst e3 e3 e1 e1)
      (iff (changeFrom' e e1)
           (exists (e2 e5)
             (and (change' e5 e1 e2) (gen e5 e) (inconsistent e3 e2))))))
