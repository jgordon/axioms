Predicate: person

Summary

(person p)

p is a person.

Characterizing Axioms

A person is a kind of agent.

(forall (p)
  (if (person p)
      (agent p)))

A person is also a kind of physical object.

(forall (p)
  (if (person p)
      (physobj p)))

A person has a body and a mind.

(forall (p)
  (if (person p)
      (exists (b m)
        (and (body b p) (mind m p)))))

Example of an axiom for a more detailed account of human physical control: A person can voluntarily lift his or her arm.

(forall (p)
  (if (person p)
      (exists (e1 e2 x)
        (and (lift' e2 p x) (arm x p) (will' e1 p e2)
             (dcause e1 e2)))))

All Axioms

A person is a kind of agent.

(forall (p)
  (if (person p)
      (agent p)))

A person is also a kind of physical object.

(forall (p)
  (if (person p)
      (physobj p)))

A person has a body and a mind.

(forall (p)
  (if (person p)
      (exists (b m)
        (and (body b p) (mind m p)))))

A person p has a set of body parts, namely, the components of the body.

(forall (p s)
  (iff (bodyPartsOf s p)
       (exists (b)
         (and (person p) (body b p) (componentsOf s b)))))

The sense organs are a subset of the body parts.

(forall (p x)
  (if (and (person p) (bodyPartsOf s1 p))
      (exists (s2)
        (and (subset s2 s1)
             (forall (o)
               (iff (member o s2) (senseOrgan o p)))))))

When something is perceived, there is a sense organ whose intact-ness enables the perception.

(forall (p x e2)
  (if (and (person p) (perceive' e2 p x))
      (exists (o e1)
        (and (senseOrgan o p) (intact' e1 o) (enable e1 e2)))))

Another subset of body parts can be directly controlled by a person's will. That is, the person's willing an event is the direct cause of the motion of the body part.

(forall (p s1)
  (if (and (person p) (bodyPartsOf s1 p))
      (exists (s2)
        (and (subset s2 s1)
             (forall (x)
               (if (member x s2)
                   (exists (e1 e2 y z)
                     (and (move' e2 x y z) (will' e1 p e2)
                          (dcause e1 e2)))))))))
