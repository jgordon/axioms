Predicate: person

Summary

(person p)

p is a person.

Characterizing Axioms

A person is a kind of agent.

(forall (p)
  (if (person p)
      (agent p)))

A person is also a kind of physical object.

(forall (p)
  (if (person p)
      (physobj p)))

A person has a body and a mind.

(forall (p)
  (if (person p)
      (exists (b m)
        (and (body b p) (mind m p)))))

Example of an axiom for a more detailed account of human physical control: A person can voluntarily lift his or her arm.

(forall (p)
  (if (person p)
      (exists (e1 e2 x)
        (and (lift' e2 p x) (arm x p) (will' e1 p e2)
             (dcause e1 e2)))))

Additional Axioms

A person p has a set of body parts, namely, the components of the body.

(forall (p s)
  (iff (bodyPartsOf s p)
       (exists (b)
         (and (person p) (body b p) (componentsOf s b)))))
