Predicate: senseOrgan

Summary

(senseOrgan o p)

o is one of p's sense organs.

Characterizing Axioms

Sense organs are body parts.

(forall (o p)
  (if (senseOrgan o p)
      (bodyPart o p)))

Additional Axioms

The sense organs are a subset of the body parts.

(forall (p x)
  (if (and (person p) (bodyPartsOf s1 p))
      (exists (s2)
        (and (subset s2 s1)
             (forall (o)
               (iff (member o s2) (senseOrgan o p)))))))
