Predicate: union

Summary

(union s s1 s2)

s is the union of s1 and s2.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (s s1 s2)
  (iff (union s s1 s2)
       (and (set s) (set s1) (set s2)
            (forall (x)
              (iff (member x s)
                   (or (member x s1) (member x s2)))))))

Additional Axioms

(forall (s s1 s2 s3)
  (iff (union3 s s1 s2 s3)
       (exists (s4)
         (and (union s s1 s4) (union s4 s2 s3)))))
