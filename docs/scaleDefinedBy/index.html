Predicate: scaleDefinedBy

Summary

(scaleDefinedBy s s1 e)

s is the scale with components s1 and partial ordering defined by relation e.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (s s1 e)
   (iff (scaleDefinedBy s s1 e)
        (and (scale s) (componentsOf s1 s)
             (exists (x y)
               (partialOrdering e x y s)))))

Additional Axioms

(forall (s s1 e x y)
   (if (and (sequence s) (componentsOf s1 s)
            (beforeInSeq' e x y s))
       (and (scaleDefinedBy s s1 e)
            (forall (z)
              (if (first z s)
                  (bottom z s))))))
