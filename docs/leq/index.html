Predicate: leq

Summary

(leq n1 n2)

n1 is less than or equal to n2.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (n1 n2)
  (iff (leq n1 n2)
       (or (lt n1 n2) (equal n1 n2))))

Additional Axioms

(forall (e)
  (iff (eventuality e)
       (exists (p n)
         (and (pred p e) (arity n e)
              (forall (i)
                (if (and (posInteger i)
                         (leq i n))
                    (exists (x)
                      (argn x i e))))))))
