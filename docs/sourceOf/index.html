Predicate: sourceOf

Summary

(sourceOf x e)

e is a change in an “at” relation and x is the location of the initial “at” relation.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (z e)
  (iff (sourceOf z e)
       (exists (x w e1 e2 s)
         (and (at' e1 x z s) (at' e2 x w s) (change' e e1 e2)))))
