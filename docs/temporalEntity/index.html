Predicate: temporalEntity

Summary

(temporalEntity t)

t is an instant or an interval.

Characterizing Axioms

A temporal entity may be an instant or an interval.

(forall (t)
  (if (temporalEntity t)
      (or (interval t) (instant t))))

A temporal entity may be an instant or an interval.

(forall (t)
  (if (temporalEntity t) (sameDuration t t)))
