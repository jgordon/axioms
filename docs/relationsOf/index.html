Predicate: relationsOf

Summary

(relationsOf s3 x)

s3 is the set of relations of composite entity x.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (s3 x)
  (if (relationsOf s3 x)
      (and (set s3) (compositeEntity x)
           (forall (e)
             (if (member e s3)
                 (exists (y z)
                   (and (componentOrWhole y x) (arg* y e)
                        (nequal z y) (arg* z e))))))))

Additional Axioms

(forall (x)
  (iff (compositeEntity x)
       (exists (s1 s2 s3)
         (and (componentsOf s1 x) (propertiesOf s2 x)
              (relationsOf s3 x)))))
