Predicate: deleteElt

Summary

(deleteElt s s1 x)

s is obtained from s1 by deleting x.

Characterizing Axioms

(forall (s s1 x)
  (iff (deleteElt s s1 x)
       (and (set s) (set s1)
            (forall (y)
              (iff (member y s)
                   (and (member y s1) (nequal y x)))))))

Additional Axioms

(forall (s s1 x y)
  (iff (replaceElt s s1 x y)
       (exists (s2)
         (and (deleteElt s2 s1 x)
              (addElt s s2 y)))))
